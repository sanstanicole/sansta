Recipe to make Colombian Guineo Verde Con Queso

  1. Take 5 Green Bananas
  2. Start boiling a pot of water
  3. Cut off the ends of the bananas
  4. Make slits long ways across the bananas
  5. Once the water is boiling, put the bananas in the pot
  6. Let them boil for about 20 minutes, or until the bananas are not too hard
  7. While waiting for them to boil, grate some Hondurenan Hard Cheese
  8. After the bananas are done boiling, take them out and take the peels off, put them on a plate
  9. Put butter on top of all of them
  10. With a fork, slightly smash all of the bananas
  11. then add the grated cheese on top
  12. Done!! :)
